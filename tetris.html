## Javascript Tetris ##

### Instructions ###

Click on the black square to start the game.
Use the arrow keys to move the falling blocks left, right, or down.
Alternately, use the w, a, s, d, keys to rotate blocks, or move them left, down, and right.
Use the z and x keys to rotate the blocks clockwise or counterclockwise.
Form continuous horizontal lines to increase the score.
Press the space bar to instantly drop a block.
Press the p key to pause or unpause the game.
If the game has ended. Press the enter key to restart if desired.
